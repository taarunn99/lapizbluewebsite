# Mapei Waterproofing in UAE: The Smart Builder's Choice for Long-Lasting Results

Water damage remains one of the most significant threats to buildings in the UAE. Despite the arid climate, intense seasonal rainfall, high groundwater salinity, humidity extremes, and condensation from air conditioning systems create constant challenges for building envelopes. Smart builders choose Mapei waterproofing solutions for proven protection.

## Why Waterproofing Matters in the UAE

The UAE's unique climate presents multiple waterproofing challenges:

- **Intense Heat**: Surface temperatures can exceed 70Â°C, stressing waterproofing membranes
- **Sudden Rainfall**: Brief but intense storms can overwhelm unprepared structures
- **Groundwater Salinity**: High salt content accelerates material degradation
- **Humidity Extremes**: Coastal areas experience significant humidity fluctuations

Without proper waterproofing, these conditions lead to structural damage, mold growth, energy inefficiency, and costly repairs.

## Essential Mapei Waterproofing Products

### Mapelastic Foundation

Designed specifically for below-grade protection, Mapelastic Foundation creates a durable barrier against groundwater and soil moisture.

**Key Features:**
- Two-component flexible cementitious membrane
- Bridges hairline cracks up to 1mm
- Resistant to carbonation and chloride penetration
- Easy roller or spray application

**Ideal Applications:**
- Basement walls and floors
- Foundation elements
- Retaining walls
- Underground structures

### Planiseal 88

A rigid cement-based coating that provides excellent protection for both positive and negative side waterproofing applications.

**Key Features:**
- Single-component, cement-based
- Can be applied to damp surfaces
- NSF certified for potable water contact
- Economical coverage rates

**Ideal Applications:**
- Water tanks and reservoirs
- Elevator pits
- Concrete pools
- Utility tunnels

### Mapelastic Smart

An ultra-flexible membrane for demanding applications where movement and crack-bridging capability are critical.

**Key Features:**
- Superior crack-bridging ability (up to 2mm)
- Excellent adhesion to various substrates
- UV resistant for exposed applications
- Fiber-reinforced formula

**Ideal Applications:**
- Terraces and balconies
- Bathrooms and wet rooms
- Swimming pool surrounds
- Rooftop installations
