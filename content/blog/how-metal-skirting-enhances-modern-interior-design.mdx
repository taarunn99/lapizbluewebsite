# How Metal Skirting Enhances Modern Interior Design?

Metal skirting boards represent a contemporary evolution of the traditional baseboard, offering functionality and style that align with modern design sensibilities. These protective boards installed at wall bases deliver both practical benefits and aesthetic advantages that elevate interior spaces.

## What is Metal Skirting?

Metal skirting refers to protective boards crafted from steel or aluminum installed where walls meet floors. Unlike traditional wood baseboards, metal skirting provides:
- Enhanced durability
- Modern aesthetic appeal
- Simplified maintenance
- Long-term performance

## Benefits of Metal Skirting

### Exceptional Durability
Metal skirting withstands daily challenges:
- Impact resistance
- Corrosion protection
- Long lifespan
- Minimal degradation

### Aesthetic Versatility
Multiple finish options suit various designs:
- Polished and brushed finishes
- Powder-coated colors
- Anodized treatments
- Custom options available

### Low Maintenance
Simplified upkeep compared to wood:
- Easy cleaning
- No painting required
- Resistant to moisture
- Long-lasting appearance

### Fire and Moisture Resistance
Safety and performance advantages:
- Non-combustible materials
- Moisture-proof surfaces
- Suitable for wet areas
- Code compliance support

## Selection Factors

### Material Choice

**Steel**
- Maximum durability
- Heavy-duty applications
- Industrial aesthetics
- Higher weight

**Aluminum**
- Lightweight installation
- Excellent corrosion resistance
- Modern appearance
- Easy fabrication

### Finish Type

**Polished**
- High reflectivity
- Contemporary glamour
- Dramatic effect
- Regular maintenance

**Brushed**
- Subtle texture
- Hides fingerprints
- Sophisticated appeal
- Easy maintenance

**Powder-Coated**
- Color variety
- Durable finish
- Design flexibility
- Custom matching

### Height Dimensions
Consider proportions for space:
- Standard heights: 60-100mm
- Tall options for high ceilings
- Low profiles for minimalist design
- Custom heights available
