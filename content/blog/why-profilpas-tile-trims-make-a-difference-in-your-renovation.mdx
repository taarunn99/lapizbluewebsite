## The Basic Functions of Tile Trims

### Edge Protection
Exposed tile edges are vulnerable to:
- Impact damage from everyday use
- Chipping and cracking over time
- Moisture infiltration at edges
- Premature wear and failure

Quality trims shield these vulnerable points, extending installation life.

### Safety Enhancement
Tile trims contribute to safer spaces:
- Cover sharp tile edges
- Reduce trip hazards at transitions
- Provide grip on stair edges
- Protect bare feet from cuts

### Professional Appearance
Nothing marks amateur work like poor edge treatment:
- Clean, finished transitions
- Consistent reveal and spacing
- Hidden cut edges
- Polished, complete look

### Hygiene Support
Proper trims contribute to cleaner environments:
- Sealed edges prevent moisture entry
- Reduced mold and mildew habitats
- Easier cleaning access
- Maintained sanitary conditions
