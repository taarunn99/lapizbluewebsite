## What Makes Ultracolor Plus Unique?

### FastTrack Ready Formula
Ultracolor Plus allows foot traffic in just three hours—a game-changer for projects where time matters. No more waiting days for grout to cure before continuing work.

### DropEffect Technology
The water-repellent DropEffect technology causes water to bead on the surface rather than absorbing into the grout. This dramatically reduces staining and simplifies maintenance.

## Mixing and Application Instructions

### Proper Mixing
- Use clean mixing containers
- Follow precise water-to-powder ratios
- Mix at low speed to minimize air incorporation
- Achieve smooth, lump-free consistency
- Allow brief slake time before remixing

### Application Technique
- Apply with quality rubber float
- Work grout into joints at diagonal angle
- Ensure complete joint filling without voids
- Remove excess before grout begins setting
- Clean with appropriate sponge and water

### Cleaning Protocol
- Begin cleaning when grout firms slightly
- Use clean water and quality sponge
- Avoid excessive water that weakens grout
- Final polish with soft, dry cloth
- Remove all haze before it hardens

## Common Issues and Prevention

### Efflorescence
White deposits can appear if mixing water contains minerals or substrate releases salts. Use clean water and ensure proper substrate preparation.

### Color Inconsistency
Variations in mixing water quantities or application techniques cause color differences. Maintain consistent procedures throughout project.

### Cracking
Hairline cracks result from substrate movement, improper mixing, or rapid drying. Ensure proper substrate preparation and curing conditions.
